Just Dance With Me

Album art for Ladies First a cappella's EP, "Just Dance With Me."

The a cappella group Ladies First decided to record an EP and this was the cover art that I worked to create for the album. I came up with the concept, design, and execution plan but was inexperieced with Adobe Illustrator at the time. With the help of Ladies First's advisor, Karl Gude, we created the final cover, which you can see below. The insert and tray card were created by myself.

November 2012
